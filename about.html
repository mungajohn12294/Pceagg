About PCEA Youth Fellowship

Our Mission

To empower, equip and inspire the youth to live Christ-centered lives, actively serve the church and the community.

Our Vision

Raising a generation of young believers grounded in faith, leadership, and transformational purpose.

Our Activities

We meet for worship, Bible studies, mentorship programs, talent showcases, charity missions, and youth-led events every month.

Be Part of Us

Join our family of creative, energetic, and faithful youths — inspiring each other to speak and live boldly for Christ!
